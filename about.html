Company overview

At ShipGoodies Transportation and Logistics, we know that time is of the essence. We have leveraged our traditional trucking services in the Mid-Atlantic and Midwest regions to shape our company today. In addition to our truckload (TL), ocean, and air transportation services, we have expanded our asset-based operations to include expedited services, reverse logistics, and volume LTL transportation; as well as a range of non-asset-based solutions to meet the needs of our customers throughout North America.

Our company values hard work, respect, commitment and teamwork, and our workplace is characterized by the high energy, enthusiasm and effort of our employees who thrive in this positive environment. Our commitment to providing first-class service to our customers begins with our employees' dedication to the businesses we serve.

Our commitment to safety and security is unparalleled. We pride ourselves on posting industry-leading Compliance, Security, Accountability (CSA) scores and industry-low claims rates, giving you and your customers peace of mind when your goods are in our hands. Our primary obligation to our customers and community begins with hiring and training our team members, and we are committed to putting the safest drivers and equipment on the road.

From our experienced drivers to our knowledgeable office staff, ShipGoodies Transportation and Logistics has the will and commitment to work together to develop a transportation solution that truly meets your needs.

We will take care of your cargo and deliver them safe and on time.

At ShipGoodies Transportation and Logistics, ensuring the safety of our customers, employees and our communities is our priority. We understand the importance of continuous training and are proud of our safety knowledge, experienced staff and ability to exceed industry standards year after year.

As a company, we have a solid safety performance history and will continue to be a leader in the area of safety and compliance.

At ShipGoodies Transportation and Logistics, we offer industry-leading asset protection and security compliance programs.

ShipGoodies Transport understands that our customers may have important and unique needs related to homeland security regulatory compliance, high-risk products, or brand protection. We offer consultation and proactive partnership to ensure that our customers’ security needs are met.

By leveraging modern and proven technologies, we provide for the integrity of customer assets while in-transit or at one of our facilities.
